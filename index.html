Clear Entries

  • Considering the density of nitrogen in the Chesapeake Bay is 5mg/L of water, that an oyster filters approximately 50 gallons of water a day, that there is between 15 and 18 trillion gallons of water in the Bay, and that there are between 10 and 500 million oysters in the Bay, it would take between 1.7 and 103 years to completely clean the Bay with the current amount of oysters and its current amount of pollution and no continued rate of pollution.
  • Considering the density of nitrogen in the Chesapeake Bay is 5mg/L of water, that an oyster filters approximately 50 gallons of water a day, that the Bay receives about 300 million pounds of pollution each year, that there is between 15 and 18 trillion gallons of water in the Bay, and that there are between 10 and 500 million oysters in the Bay, it would take between 2.9 and 10697 years to completely clean the Bay with the current amount of oysters and its current amount of pollution and its continued rate of pollution.

Calculations

Water filtered / Yr / Oyster

50 * 350 = 17500

This is the amount of water a single oyster filters per year, in gallons.

Time Needed to Clean the Bay With Its Current Amount of Pollution and Its Continued Rate of Pollution

Low End --- (15,000,000,000,000 / (500,000,000 * 17,500)) + (1.7 * 15,000,000,000,000 / (500,000,000 * 17,500))

This is how many years it will take for the Bay to be completely cleaned with the high end of the estimation of how many oysters are in the Bay, and the low end of how much water is in the Chesapeake Bay.

High End --- (18,000,000,000,000 / (10,000,000 * 17,500)) + (103 * 18,000,000,000,000 / (10,000,000 * 17,500))

This is how many years it will take for the Bay to be completely cleaned with the low end of the estimation of how many oysters are in the Bay, and the high end of how much water is in the Chesapeake Bay.

This is how many years it will take for the Bay to be completely cleaned with the low end of the estimation of how many oysters are in the Bay, and the high end of how much water is in the Chesapeake Bay, but with no continued rate of pollution.

Oysters Needed to Clean the Bay in X Amount of Time With Its Current Amount of Pollution and Its Continued Rate of Pollution
